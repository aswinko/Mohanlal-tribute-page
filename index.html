Latest Films

Marakkar Arabikadalinte Simham

‘Marakkar Arabikadalinte Simham’ is an upcoming 100 crore-budget Malayalam movie that is directed by Priyadarshan. Co-written by Ani Sasi, the film is releasing in 5 Indian languages which include Malayalam, Tamil, Telugu, Hindi, and Kannada. ‘Marakkar Lion of the Arabian Sea’ portrays the courageous life-events of a rebellious naval chief, Kunjali Marakkar the fourth, who fought against the Portuguese in the ancient times.

Aaraattu

Aaraattu is set in a fictional village called Muthalakotta. The rustic serenity of the place is rattled by the arrival of a stranger, Neyyattinkara Gopan, who is a rare mix of bravado and comic extravagance. The very purpose of his coming to the village undermines what the people there stand for. But their resistance withers layer by layer as Gopan weaves his spell on them. He is at once a hero and villain. It takes only a while for him to “control” the villagers. As they succumb to his devious design, little do they know what is kept in store for them!

Drishyam 2

'A crime once done, haunts forever.' It has been six years since that fateful night. Georgekutty and family are still living in its trauma. Rani feels Georgekutty has changed, and is no more the same person he was. Georgekutty who owns a theatre now and is after his dream of producing a film. Will this new aspiration endanger his family? The film explores three aspects: a family who is living in the fear of being caught one day; Geeta and Prabhakar, the parents who lost their son, still trying to find justice; and the Police department still desperately trying to solve the puzzle.

Lucifer

After a long time does Mohanlal come with a political thriller. ‘Lucifer’, the movie is directed by Super Star Prithviraj Sukumaran who wears the director-cap for the first time. This itself reveals clear that the very project is from a stellar combo. ‘Lucifer’ portrays the story of brotherhood and betrayal, followed by extreme and unending vengeance. The teaser of the movie had already left millions of viewers with awe and terror. The film is written by Murali Gopi and produced by Antony Perumbavoor under the banner of Aashirvad Cinemas. Gopi Sundar has composed its songs.

Big Brother

Big Brother is a Malayalam action-thriller directed by Siddique. This big-budget movie which has Mohanlal in the lead role is a mixture of romance, drama, action and comedy. Arbaaz Khan enters Malayalam Film Industry for the first time through the film, in the role of an IPS officer. Big Brother centers around various incidents highlighted by the bond of brotherhood. Produced jointly by S Talkies, Shaman International, and Vaishaka Cynyma, in association with Carnival Movie Network, the film’s front roles are played by Sarjano Khalid, Mirna Menon, Anoop Menon, Siddique, Honey Rose, Vishnu Unnikrishnan, and Tini Tom.
